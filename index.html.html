TOP 10 LARGEST DOG BREEDS




In the canine world there are some dogs that are notorious for being very large.

These dogs have literally broken the scale when weighing these behemoth canines and some can even be larger than their human companions. So lets take a look at the "TOP 10 LARGEST DOG BREEDS IN THE WORLD".




10.

The Kangal sheperd dog is the tenth largest dog on the planet and can reach an impressive weight of over 160 ibs. These dogs were breed in Turkey for the purpose of guarding livestock such as Sheep from other wild beasts such as Wolves. The dogs also hold the record of having the strongest bite in the canine universe, at over 700psi these dogs possess a stronger bite than even a lion which has a bite force of about 650psi. All though these dogs are independent they are also very loving and affectionate towards their family. They are not suitable for novice or inexperienced owners as they can be quite difficult to train



9.

At the number 9 spot is the Leonberger which can clock in at a weight of over 170 ibs. These dogs were bred as working and rescue dogs and though they might look intimidating are actually calm, loving and affectionate dogs. Unfortunately due to their large size are prone to diseases and have a rather short lifespan of about 7 years.



8.

Next we have the Irish wolfhound which can clock in at a weight of 170 ibs. This dog is was bred in ireland as a sight hound dog for hunters. The dog also holds the record for the tallest dog in the world and can reach a height of 32 inches at the shoulders and can be as tall as 7 ft when standing on it's hind legs.



7.

Next on the list we have the new foundland which can clock in at a weight of 180 ibs. These dogs originated from the island of New foundland and where bred as a rescue dog. These dogs possess a water resistant coat and strong paws which makes them execellent swimmers. Their large size also gives them buoyancy on water. They have been used for centuries to rescue people and retrieve object on water.



6.

One of my personal favorite breeds the neapolitan mastiff which can weight between 185-190 ibs. These dogs originated from Italy and were bred as guard dogs for families. The dog just like other mastiff dog breeds is a descendant of the ancient and now extinct molossus and breed of war dog from greece used for protection and gladiator fights. Just like their antestor these dogs possess a large head and protective instinct. They are easily identified by their wrinkly face. The are stubborn, independent, difficult to train and not suitable for novince or first time owners

5.

Another one of my favorite breeds the Great dane takes the number 5 spot on our list and can weigh as much as 200 1bs 91 kg. Although thought by some to originate from denmark this behemoth hound actually originates from Germany. It is a cross between the Irish wolfhound and English mastiff which are both ginormous dogs in their own right. This hell of a hound was bred for hunting big game such as Wild boar but today are gaint friendly couch potatoes that are calm gentle and loving to their families. Great danes are also believed to have the ability to chase away ghosts and evils spirits afterall scooby doo is a greatdane. Unfortunately just like the leonberger the Great dane also has a relatively short lifespan of 6-8 years but these dogs will give loads of love and affection on their days on earth.



4.

Number on our list is Boerbel. Also known in dutch as "farm dog" this massive dog breed clocks on at a weight of 220 ibs. This dog has it's origins in south Africa and was bred to the guard homes and livestock from predators like Hyenas and even Lions. They are stubborn, independent, confident, loyal and devoted to their families. Due to their protective and territorial nature Boerbels make excellent guard dogs which also makes them weary of strangers and aggressive to individuals their are not farmiliar with but with proper training and socialization they can be great, loving and devoted pets.



3.

Lets look at the biggest hounds in the game. At number 3 we have the Caucasian shepherd which can weigh as much as 220 ibs. This dog has it's origins in the over 7 different countries namely Russia, Turkey, Georgia, Aremnia, Azerbaijan, Soviet Union and Circassia. This dog was bred as a shepherd dog for guard livestock and their families from predators such as Wolves and even Bears. Their thick coat protects thems from intense cold and harsh winters. With proper socialization and training these canine gaints makes great family pets and are great with kids and other pets.



2.

Number 2 on our list is the English mastiff which can weigh as much as 230 ibs. This dog is the largest dog ever and due to it's name has it's origins in England and date as far back as 3000bc. During the roman invasion caeser was so impressed by the dogs that he took some home with him. The dogs became a war and fighting dog as they were used for bull baiting and fighting against Lions, Tigers and Human gladiators. Today these dogs are a large and loving family pet and require lots of grooming, love, and food. The largest of these dogs ever recorded was an English mastiff named Zorba which weighed in at a massive 317 ibs WOW!!!.Check out this pic of Zorba



1.

Finally at number one on our list is the Saint bernard which can weigh a massive 230 ibs. These humongous hounds originated from the Swiss alpes where they bred as rescue dogs. The Saint bernard was used mainly to rescue people trapped in snow. Their large bodies and thick coat enabled them to navigate through snow easily. A Saint bernard named Barry who lived in the monastery from 1800-1812 saved over 40 people and in 1815 his dead body was displayed in the national museum of Berne, Switzerland where it remains today. The largest of these dog was a dog named Benedict who weighed a mind blowing 357 ibs INSANE!!!.
